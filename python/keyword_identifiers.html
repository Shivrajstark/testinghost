Keywords

  • Python keywords are special reserved words that have specific meanings and purposes and can't be used for anything but those specific purposes

  • Keywords are the vocabulary that helps you instruct the interpreter on how to perform specific actions, make decisions, and structure your program

Identifiers

  • An identifier is the name assigned to a variable, function, class, or other object. It is used to refer to the object in the program and is a way to give the object a human-readable name.
  • Choosing meaningful and descriptive identifiers is a way of making your code readable and self-explanatory.
